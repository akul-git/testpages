Ours has a Pune-based tech company that’s been around since 1994. Specialize in building custom software, offering IT consulting, and helping other companies hire the right tech talent. Think of us as a mix between a software development firm and a staffing partner. We worked with big names like Amdocs, Tech Mahindra, FIS Global, Emerson, and Wolters Kluwer — so we are not just doing small-time projects. Our clients span industries like telecom, finance, engineering, and enterprise software.

We don’t just recruit — we also train candidates before placing them, especially when clients need people who are ready to hit the ground running.
